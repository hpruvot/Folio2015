01.

The brief

Dyskit is a music platform that allows unknown artists to get known faster. There is a voting process when you listen to a track so the artist can find out his popularity.

We had to think and design the whole UX/UI in a very short time and it might be the biggest challenge I had.

A cross device service

Grid & layers

The grid used is a full-width layer in order to use the whole space of the browser. The left sidebar is the menu, the right content is the audio player, and the centered content displays everything you need to know about artists and songs.
